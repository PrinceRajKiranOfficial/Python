Matplotlib is a powerful **data visualization library** in Python.

With Matplotlib, you can create a variety of charts such as:  
- Line plots  
- Bar charts  
- Scatter plots  
- Histograms  
- Pie charts  

It also allows **full customization** and fine control over every element of a plot.
